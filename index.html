Services

  • Eyelash Extensions

    Eyelash Extensions is a practice that enhances the length, thickness and fullness of your natural eyelashes. Once selected, the lashes are applied one at a time using a specially formulated, semi-permanent glue that will not irritate the eye nor damage the natural lash.…

  • Microblading (3D Brows)

    Microblading, Micro-pigmentaiton, 3D eyebrows or Eyebrow embroidery is a relatively new, manual method. It is considered to be semi-permanent, as compared to the traditional hairstroke technique. It is done using a very fine blade to deposit pigments into the epidermis.…

  • Lash & Brow Tinting

    Tinting gives the eyes definition and lets you go without mascara, at least for a few weeks until eyelashes fall out and are replaced by new ones, You shut your eyes, and the tint is applied to lashes with a fine brush, and allowed to set for at least 10 minutes.…

  • Eyebrow Extensions
