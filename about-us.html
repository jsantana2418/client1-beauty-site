Joeimy's first entrepreneurial experience was when she opened Remote Paralegal Solutions LLC, a small boutique offering assistance to attorneys, translation services, and individual tax preparation. After years of Paralegal and Social work she decided to switch her career after her involvement with modeling, fashion and pageants. This entrepreneur is the founder of Latinas Boston Business Group, a closed group for Women to Empower, Network, and much more.

Joeimy has a Bachelor's Degree in Criminal Justice from Northeastern University; she is a Graduate of the Catherine Hinds Institute of Esthetics, A Massachusetts licensed Aesthetician, a Certified Eyelash Extension Artisan and a Micro-pigmentation Technician. Her consummate professionalism, passion, knowledge, and understanding of this ever-evolving industry led her to the master of all techniques. She believes that the infinite path to perfection is paved with the satisfaction of each and every client. Her true artistry, warm personality and genuine desire to work and relate to each client and achieve optimum results is what separates her from other professionals in the industry. She is vigilant in implementing her high standards.

Joeimy has appeared on a number of publications including Candela Magazine's 2015 list of the Top 25 Most Attractive and Influential Latinos, so it should be of no surprise that she also won Boston's Miss Latin Beauty Pageant in 2013 as Miss Dominican Republic (Miss Belleza Latina Boston). Throughout the competition she was fortunate to have worked with some of Boston's top makeup artists and beauty professionals and as a result became fascinated with the painstaking details associated with enhancing one’s natural beauty.

Joeimy is also working as a Medical Aesthetician at Boston Laser & Cosmetic Center and as a Microblading (Permanent Cosmetics) Technician at Dream Medical Spa.
